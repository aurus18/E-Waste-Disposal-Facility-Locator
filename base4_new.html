Locate, Recycle, Repeat: Find E-Waste Centers

Think of us as your E-waste GPS. We'll guide you to the nearest facilities for responsible electronic waste disposal.

The search bar allows users to input their location, and upon searching, it provides a list or map of nearby E-waste facilities. This feature helps users easily locate and access E-waste recycling centers in their vicinity, promoting responsible electronic waste disposal and contributing to a cleaner environment.
